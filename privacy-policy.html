Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

Effective date: 1 June 2026  ·  Last updated: 1 June 2026  ·  Applies to: hearitright.com and all subdomains

1. Who We Are

HearITright Technology Services OPC Private Limited ("HearITright", "we", "us", or "our") is a technology consulting company incorporated in India (CIN: U72900TN2017OPC114153.), with its registered office in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India.

For the purposes of applicable data protection law — including India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 (DPDP Act) and, where applicable, the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) — HearITright is the Data Fiduciary / Data Controller in respect of personal data collected through this website.

2. Personal Data We Collect

We collect personal data only when you actively provide it to us. We use third-party analytics and visitor intelligence tools to understand how this website is used and to improve the experience for visitors. These tools may collect data about your visit automatically, as described below.

2.1 Contact and Enquiry Data

When you submit our contact form, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Company name (optional)
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Service of interest
  • Description of your requirement

2.2 Job Application Data

When you submit a job application, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address and phone number
  • Employment history, qualifications, and portfolio information you choose to share
  • Any other information included in your application

2.3 Technical Data

When you visit our website, your browser makes standard HTTP requests which may include your IP address and browser type. This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. We do not operate server-side logs independently, but GitHub's infrastructure may record this data in accordance with GitHub's Privacy Statement.

This website loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). When fonts are fetched, Google's servers receive your IP address and browser information. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

2.4 Analytics and Visitor Intelligence Data

When you visit our website, the following tools may automatically collect data about your visit:

  • Google Analytics — collects anonymised data including pages visited, time on site, browser type, device type, and approximate location derived from IP address. IP anonymisation is enabled.
  • Zoho SalesIQ — a visitor intelligence and live chat tool that may collect your IP address, browser information, pages visited, time on site, and any information you voluntarily provide through the chat interface. We may use SalesIQ's full feature set, including visitor identification and lead intelligence capabilities.
  • We may add further analytics, advertising, or visitor intelligence tools over time — including but not limited to Meta Pixel and other third-party platforms. This section will be updated when such tools are added. The types of data collected will be consistent with the categories described above.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use personal data only for the purpose for which it was collected:

  • Enquiry data — to respond to your enquiry, assess whether we can help, and (where relevant) follow up on a proposal or engagement.
  • Application data — to evaluate your application, contact you regarding potential opportunities, and retain your profile for future consideration with your consent.
  • Technical data — for website operation and security only.
  • Analytics and visitor intelligence data — to understand how visitors use our website, identify areas for improvement, and where you initiate a chat conversation, to respond to your enquiry.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We do not use your data for automated decision-making or profiling.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Under the DPDP Act 2023 (India)

We process your personal data on the basis of consent given at the time of form submission, and for legitimate uses as defined under the Act — specifically, responding to a communication initiated by you and fulfilling purposes reasonably expected in the context of that communication.

Under the GDPR (EU/EEA/UK)

Where GDPR applies, our legal bases are:

  • Legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f)) — for responding to business enquiries and managing potential client relationships.
  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a)) — for job application data and any marketing communications.
  • Pre-contractual measures (Article 6(1)(b)) — where enquiry data relates to an imminent service engagement.

5. Third-Party Data Processors

We use the following third-party services that process personal data on our behalf:

  • Zoho Corporation Pvt. Ltd. — All forms on our website are powered by Zoho Forms. Form submissions are stored on Zoho's servers. Zoho is ISO 27001 certified and processes data in accordance with its Privacy Policy. Data may be stored in India and/or the United States.
  • Zoho Corporation Pvt. Ltd. — Zoho SalesIQ is used for visitor intelligence and live chat. See Zoho's Privacy Policy for details.
  • Google LLC — Google Fonts are loaded from Google's CDN. Google may process your IP address and browser data when delivering font files.
  • Google LLC — Google Analytics is used to collect anonymised website usage data. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.
  • GitHub, Inc. — This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may process standard server request data.

We do not share your personal data with any other third parties except as required by law or with your explicit consent.

6. Data Retention

  • Enquiry data — retained for up to 24 months from the date of last contact, or longer if an active client relationship exists.
  • Job application data — retained for up to 24 months from submission if no engagement follows, to allow consideration for future opportunities. You may request deletion at any time.
  • Active client data — retained for the duration of the engagement and for 7 years thereafter, as required for tax, legal, and audit purposes under Indian law.

7. International Data Transfers

Our primary operations are in India. Data submitted through Zoho Forms may be stored or processed outside India (including in the United States). Zoho maintains appropriate safeguards for cross-border data transfers. If you are located in the EU/EEA and your data is transferred outside the EEA, such transfers are governed by applicable Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent mechanisms.

8. Your Rights

Under the DPDP Act 2023 (India)

As a Data Principal, you have the right to:

  • Obtain a summary of personal data we hold about you and how it is being processed
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data
  • Erasure of personal data where processing is no longer necessary for the stated purpose
  • Grievance redressal — to raise a complaint with us and, if unresolved, to the Data Protection Board of India
  • Nominate another person to exercise these rights on your behalf in the event of your death or incapacity

Under the GDPR (EU/EEA/UK)

You have the right to: access your data; rectification of inaccurate data; erasure ("right to be forgotten"); restriction of processing; data portability; object to processing; and to withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

How to exercise your rights

Email us at info@hearitright.com with the subject line "Data Rights Request". We will respond within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

9. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. All data submitted through our forms is transmitted over HTTPS. Our forms are processed by Zoho, which maintains its own security certifications.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. If you become aware of a security concern relating to your data, please contact us immediately at info@hearitright.com.

10. Cookies

This website uses cookies. Please see our Cookie Policy for full details on the types of cookies used, their purpose, and how to manage your preferences.

11. Children's Privacy

This website is directed at businesses and professionals. We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under the age of 18. If you believe a minor has submitted personal data to us, please contact us so we can delete it.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be noted with an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of this website after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
