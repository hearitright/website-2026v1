Our Services

Three specialisations. One trusted partner.

Specialisation 01

IT Infrastructure

We design, deploy and manage technology environments that scale with your business. From initial scoping to ongoing support, we ensure your technology is always optimised, secure and running.

Managed IT Support

Multi-tier support covering software licensing, patch management, ITIL-aligned best practices, and remote and on-site technical assistance.

Collaborative Workplace Solutions

Secure business email, productivity apps, cloud storage, and email migration services to keep your team connected and productive.

Contract Lifecycle Management

End-to-end contract workflows including negotiation support and legally binding e-signature solutions built on Zoho Contracts and Zoho Sign.

Finance & Operations Management

Implementation of Zoho Finance Suite covering accounting, expense management, inventory, and project management. Implementation and consulting only.

Secure Cloud Infrastructure

Bare metal servers, virtual private servers, cPanel and Plesk environments, elastic storage and compute, and Desktop as a Service.

Asset Lifecycle Management

From requirement scoping and procurement through to support, maintenance, asset allocation policy, depreciation management, and secure sanitisation.

Specialisation 02

Data Security & Resilience

We take a proactive approach to security — identifying and closing vulnerabilities before they become incidents. Our security framework is built into every solution we deliver, not added as an afterthought.

Device & Endpoint Protection

Comprehensive protection across all endpoints including threat detection and response, managed detection, and remote monitoring and management.

Email Security & Data Loss Prevention

Email threat protection, DLP across servers, virtual machines, workstations and hosting environments, and email archiving for Microsoft 365.

Backup & Disaster Recovery

Backup coverage for servers, virtual machines, NAS, workstations and hosted environments. Microsoft 365 and Google Drive backup. Full disaster recovery planning and execution.

Policy Definition & Management

Firewall policy design and management, centralised threat monitoring, IT and InfoSec policy frameworks, financial administration policy, and ongoing policy adherence auditing.

Identity Governance

Identity lifecycle management, biometric authentication for endpoints and web services, IAM and PAM, and SAML and SSO authentication.
